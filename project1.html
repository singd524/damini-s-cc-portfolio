Assignment #1 — Lost and Found

Project Overview

Partner for assignment: Zainab Vajihi

The focus was on the vivid Mediterranean style of Santa Barbara, emphasizing the big blue sky, scattered clouds, and colorful shops. Color was crucial to signify the unique architecture and make the piece recognizable. My interpretation centered on translating the partner's fond memory of lush green trees, colorful facades, and clear skies into a static, aesthetic composition.

Technical Details

  • Core Composition: Utilized basic geometric shapes (`rect()`, `ellipse()`, `triangle()`) combined with precise coordinate placement to represent the architectural composition.
  • Color Variation: Employed the `random()` function to generate and assign different color schemes to the architectural elements upon refresh, symbolizing the changing light and memory distortion.
  • Static Composition: Focused on a fixed perspective without the `draw()` loop to capture the aesthetic and focal points of the memory.
