Assignment #6 — Data Portrait

Project Overview

For this data portrait, I tracked my daily steps to see if I could consistently achieve 10,000 steps, exploring patterns in my walking habits and motivation levels. I learned that my walking is generally consistent, though weekends vary. This project made me more mindful of my physical activity and motivated me to be strategic about fitting walks into my day.

Technical Details

  • Data Preparation: Employed arrays and loops to store and iterate through the collected daily step data, managing the values prior to visualization.
  • Data Mapping: Used the `map()` function extensively to translate raw step counts (e.g., 5,000 to 12,000 steps) into visual properties like the size, color saturation, or position of the visual elements, creating the abstract portrait.
  • Visualization Design: Used the data points to control the composition of the sketch, representing consistency and variation in walking habits through structured patterns and subtle shifts in the generated form.
